What We Offer

Notary Services

Comprehensive legal notarization services for all your personal and professional documentation requirements.

01 📄
Document Notarization

Official attestation of legal documents, verifying authenticity and proper execution in accordance with applicable law.

02 📋
Affidavits

Drafting and notarizing sworn written statements for use in court proceedings, personal matters, and official applications.

03 🤝
Agreements & Contracts

Notarization of rental agreements, partnership deeds, service contracts, and all manner of legally binding agreements.

04 🏠
Property Documents

Authentication of sale deeds, gift deeds, lease agreements, and all real estate related legal documentation.

05 🪪
Identity Verification

Formal verification of identity documents for use in government, financial, and legal proceedings.

06 ⚖️
Legal Declarations

Notarization of statutory declarations, undertakings, indemnity bonds, and other formal legal statements.

07 🗂️
Certification of Copies

Certifying true copies of original documents including educational certificates, passports, and official records.

08 ✍️
Power of Attorney

Drafting and notarizing General and Special Power of Attorney documents for personal and business purposes.
